# Prompts SORA — Alcool de Viande

Direction artistique : ultra-luxe, sombre, minimal. Références : campagnes Louis XIII Cognac, The Macallan, Dom Pérignon. Fond noir dominant, éclairage dramatique au clair-obscur, touches d'or et d'ambre. Aucun texte sur les images.

---

## 1. Hero — Image d'ambiance principale

```
Extremely dark, cinematic photograph of a single crystal decanter filled with a pale amber liquid, placed alone on a polished black marble surface. A single focused spotlight from above creates a halo of warm golden light on the liquid, while everything else falls into near-total darkness. Wisps of fine mist or condensation surround the base. The mood is reverent, ceremonial, almost sacred. Think: Louis XIII cognac campaign meets Dutch Golden Age still life. Ultra high-end luxury spirits aesthetic. No text, no labels. Shot on Phase One IQ4, 150MP quality.
```

## 2. La Matière — Foie gras cru

```
Dramatic close-up photograph of a single whole raw duck foie gras on a dark slate surface. The liver has that characteristic smooth, pale-pink marbled surface with golden fat veins. Lit from one side with warm, concentrated light against a completely black background. The treatment is that of a precious gemstone or museum specimen — clinical reverence. A few coarse salt crystals scattered nearby catch the light. Style: Caravaggio meets Michelin-star food photography. No hands, no garnish, no text.
```

## 3. Le Procédé — Hydrolyse enzymatique

```
Dark laboratory photograph of a glass Erlenmeyer flask containing a cloudy, pale golden liquid (liver hydrolysate), sitting on a magnetic stirrer hot plate. The flask is lit from behind, making the liquid glow warmly. Surrounding darkness. In the soft background, blurred shapes of copper tubing and glass apparatus. The light is warm amber, not clinical white. Style: artisanal distillery meets biochemistry lab. Moody, intentional, beautiful. No people, no text.
```

## 4. Le Procédé — L'alambic

```
Cinematic photograph of a small traditional Charentais copper pot still (alambic) in a dark cellar. The copper surfaces reflect warm golden-amber light from a single overhead source. A thin stream of clear distillate drops from the serpentine into a glass receiving flask. The background is pure darkness with just the faintest texture of stone walls. Steam or vapour catches the light. Atmosphere: ancient craft, precious output. Think: whisky distillery photography at its most dramatic. No people, no text.
```

## 5. Le Produit — La bouteille

```
Ultra-premium product photography of a single minimalist bottle on a black background. The bottle is heavy clear glass with an angular, architectural silhouette — no label, no decoration except a small embossed mark. It contains a very pale amber, almost golden liquid. Lit from behind and slightly below to make the liquid glow. The cap is a simple matte black cylinder. The surface it rests on is barely visible — polished obsidian or black lacquer. One single reflection. Absolute minimalism, absolute luxury. Think: Tom Ford fragrance meets rare whisky cask strength bottling. No text.
```

## 6. Dégustation — Le verre

```
Intimate, moody photograph of a Glencairn tasting glass half-filled with pale amber spirit, held at a slight tilt by a male hand in a dark suit sleeve with a gold cufflink visible. Completely black background. The liquid catches a warm spotlight, glowing like liquid gold. Shallow depth of field — the fingers and glass rim are sharp, everything else fades to darkness. Style: fine whisky tasting photography, but more mysterious, more exclusive. No face visible. No text.
```

## 7. Les foies — Sélection

```
Overhead documentary photograph of five whole raw duck foie gras arranged in a precise row on a dark wooden surface, each with a small handwritten paper tag (illegible numbers). A pair of white-gloved hands holds calipers measuring one of the livers. Clinical precision meets luxury food sourcing. Lit by a single warm overhead light, everything outside the row falls into shadow. Style: auction house specimen inspection meets food photography. Serious, methodical, premium. No readable text.
```

---

## Direction artistique — Résumé

| Paramètre | Directive |
|---|---|
| **Fond** | Noir ou quasi-noir systématique |
| **Éclairage** | Clair-obscur dramatique, source chaude unique, jamais de flash frontal |
| **Palette** | Noir (#0a0a09), or/ambre (#c5a55a), cuivre, touches d'oxblood (#5c2020) |
| **Style** | Intersection entre campagne Hennessy Paradis et photographie de musée |
| **Textures** | Cuivre poli, verre épais, marbre noir, ardoise, cuir |
| **Aucun texte** | Labels et marque ajoutés en post/CSS |
| **Format** | 16:9 pour hero, 3:4 pour matière/produit, 1:1 pour procédé |
| **Références** | Louis XIII, The Macallan Genesis, Dom Pérignon Vintage, Caravaggio |
